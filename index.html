Our Services

Comprehensive care under one roof

From routine check-ups to emergency surgery, our experienced team provides the full spectrum of healthcare services your family needs.

Emergency & Urgent Care

24/7 emergency response with experienced physicians ready when you need them most.

Maternity & Newborn

Comprehensive prenatal, delivery, and postnatal care in a warm, modern maternity unit.

Orthopedics

Expert treatment for bone, joint, and muscle conditions. Weekly specialist clinics.

Laboratory & Diagnostics

Full haemogram, urinalysis, ECG, and advanced diagnostic testing under one roof.

Women’s Health

Gynecology, breast health, urogynaecology, and holistic care for every stage of life.

Child Welfare

Well-baby visits, growth monitoring, vaccinations, and pediatric consultations.

Physiotherapy

Rehabilitation and recovery programs to restore mobility and improve quality of life.

Inpatient & Surgery

Spacious private wards, intensive care, and surgical services with compassionate nursing.
